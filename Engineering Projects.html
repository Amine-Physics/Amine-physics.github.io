Energy Storage & Statistical Modelling

Bayesian Hierarchical Forecasting of Battery Cycle Life
Bayesian Statistics · Optimization · Energy Storage · 2026

This capstone project develops a probabilistic framework for forecasting lithium-ion battery cycle life under different charging policies. Using Bayesian hierarchical negative binomial regression on the Severson et al. LFP/graphite dataset (124 cells across 3 batches), the analysis quantifies uncertainty in battery degradation and enables risk-aware policy selection for grid-scale energy storage systems.

Key contributions:

  • Hierarchical model accounting for batch heterogeneity and manufacturing variability
  • MCMC convergence diagnostics (Gelman-Rubin, effective sample size, trace plots)
  • Probabilistic predictions: conservative policy achieves 57% probability of ≥1000 cycles vs. 39% for aggressive policy
  • Interactive Power BI dashboard for policy comparison and stakeholder communication
  • Model validation on held-out test data with residual analysis

Technical approach: JAGS-based MCMC sampling with 300,000 iterations, three-chain ensemble, hierarchical priors on batch intercepts and coefficients, interaction term between charging and discharging rates, prior sensitivity analysis.

Fluid Dynamics & Heat Transfer

Thermal Transport in Cylindrical Channels
Heat Transfer · Fluid Dynamics · 2026

This project investigates thermal transport in cylindrical channels under laminar flow conditions. The work combines analytical modelling with numerical simulations to study temperature evolution, heat diffusion, and the influence of flow parameters on thermal efficiency. Particular emphasis is placed on the coupling between fluid motion and heat transfer, with applications to engineering systems involving cooling, microfluidics, and thermal management.
