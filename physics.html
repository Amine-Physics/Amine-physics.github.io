Notes on Quantum Field theory of gravity

These notes are compiled during my study of the book *An Introduction to Covariant Quantum Gravity and Asymptotic Safety* by Dr. Roberto Percacci. Their aim is to develop an understanding of both the strengths and the limitations of quantum field theoretic approaches to gravity. My motivation for studying this topic originates from my master’s thesis work. While exploring how quantum field theoretic techniques can simplify calculations and provide a natural framework for describing interactions in gravitational theories, I began to wonder whether these methods could offer more than computational tools, and instead provide genuine insight into the structure of quantum gravity itself. At the time of writing, I have covered the first two chapters of the book. These notes will continue to be updated as my studies progress. Open PDF
