Fluid Dynamics & Heat Transfer

Thermal Transport in Cylindrical Channels
Heat Transfer · Fluid Dynamics · 2026

This project investigates thermal transport in cylindrical channels under laminar flow conditions. The work combines analytical modelling with numerical simulations to study temperature evolution, heat diffusion, and the influence of flow parameters on thermal efficiency. Particular emphasis is placed on the coupling between fluid motion and heat transfer, with applications to engineering systems involving cooling, microfluidics, and thermal management.
